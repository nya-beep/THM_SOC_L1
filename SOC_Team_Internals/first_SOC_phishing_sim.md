#Phishing Simulator

Today I completed my first hands on activity on TryHackMe. The title of the room was "Introduction to Phishing".
The room simulated a mock SIEM dashboard with 5 incoming phishing alerts and my job was to detect and properly triage 
the 3 true positive alerts. 

There were a total of 5 alerts, but I was able to find all 3 true positives and passed with a 100% success rate! 
However, I need to make some improvements when it comes to writing reports. My logic when it comes to understanding the 
alert is there, but my reports could use more detail. I should keep the 5 W's (who, what, when, where, and why)
in mind when writing the reports in the future.
