# Alert Triage Basics

## What I Learned
Alert triaging is the process of determining the priority and severity of alerts as they come through on the SIEM dashboard.

## Important Concepts
- Not every alert is malicious, there are often false positives 
- It's crucial for security analysts to be able to determine severity and urgency when it comes to alerts. 
- Leaving comments is essential when closing alerts as it gives context and reasoning for an analyst's decisions

## Example Questions Analysts Ask
- Is this normal behavior?
- Has this happened before?
- What system or user is affected?
- Does this activity match known attack behavior?

## Why This Matters
Efficient triage helps security teams focus on real threats quickly.

## Reflection
I learned that SOC analysts spend a lot of time evaluating alerts rather than immediately assuming every alert is an attack.
